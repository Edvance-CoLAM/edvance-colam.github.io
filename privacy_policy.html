About Edvance

Edvance is a learning analytics tool developed at the Centre for Learning Analytics at Monash University. It is designed for research and educational improvement purposes only and is not developed for commercial or profit-making use.

Edvance is implemented as a Chrome extension that provides students with feedback and insights based on their learning activity within Learning Management Systems (LMSs) such as Moodle or Ed.

The extension collects only user interaction logs within the extension itself to support the generation of feedback and insights. It does not collect any information about user activity outside of the extension.

Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

Edvance uses your personal data exported from LMS platforms. These personal data have been securely stored in password-protected storage systems. These data include your interaction records on the LMS platform, your grades, username, full name, email, etc.

Usage Data

Edvance collects only interaction logs that occur within the Chrome extension itself. This includes actions like clicking buttons, inspecting visualizations or interacting with feedback provided by the extension.

It does not collect data from other browser tabs, websites, or applications outside of the extension environment.

User-side Data Storage

We use the local storage of Google Chrome to store information required to maintain the functionality of the extension. This includes the token of the logged-in user, the name of the user and the learning analytics insights for the user.

Use of Your Personal Data

We use your personal data solely for educational and research purposes, specifically using only the data of users who have signed up to participate in the research projects. It includes:

  • To provide real-time feedback and insights through the Edvance extension to support learning.
  • To improve the educational experience and better understand learner behaviors within LMS platforms.
  • To support academic research in the fields of learning analytics, education technology, and digital pedagogy.
  • To manage Your Account and preferences within the extension (if applicable).
  • To ensure secure, effective operation of the Edvance platform and monitor usage in aggregate.

No commercial profiling, advertising, or marketing is conducted using Your data.

Sharing of Your Personal Data

Your personal data is handled with confidentiality and care. It may be shared only in the following academic and research-related contexts:

  • With Monash University researchers and technical staff supporting the development and improvement of Edvance.
  • With affiliated academic partners or research collaborators, under strict ethical guidelines and anonymization protocols.
  • With data analysis teams, only in aggregated or anonymized formats, to study usage trends and educational impact.
  • With Your explicit consent, for any other purposes or participation in educational research studies.

We do not share Your data with advertisers, commercial entities, or unrelated third parties.
